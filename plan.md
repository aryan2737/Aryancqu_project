## Communication Plan

| Name of Meeting | Participants | Purpose | Frequency | Type |
|-----------------|--------------|---------|-----------|------|
| Project Planning Session |  (Individual) | To plan tasks, milestones, and timelines | Once at project start | Self-planned |
| Weekly Progress Review |  (Individual) | To review completed work and identify pending tasks | Weekly | Self-review |
| Design Review Session |  (Individual) | To review and refine network diagrams and designs | As needed | Self-review |
| Documentation Review |  (Individual) | To check accuracy and completeness of written work | Before submission | Self-review |
| Final Submission Check |  (Individual) | To ensure all requirements are met before submission | Once | Self-review |
